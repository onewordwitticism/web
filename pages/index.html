About me

I've had a bit of a round-about education; first focusing on exercise and physiology which then branched out into some clinical motor-neuroscience research. I think this is where my first interest for complex systems began, but unfortunately didn't have the programming or mathematics skills to really delve into the area like I wanted/want too. I made the leap, and went to study mathematics and programming, and now here I am logging my work whilst planning my next steps into the industry.

Maybe I got interested in complexity, or more that it was impressed on me, due to the area that I grew up in. South-East Queensland has some beautiful national parks and natural scenery, which can leave a strong impression on a young adult of the vastness and scale of ecological systems (See some photos here). It almost seems a shame, or a surprise, that a video game hasn't been produced using Australia's natural flora and fauna which is all quite unique.

Another interest I've developed recently is due to one thing I love; which is electronic music. With the advent of the internet there it's opened the door to creators projecting their work out at scale, and there is a huge abundance of quality music which seemingly lacks visuals which tie into the music well. Although complex, I'd love to attempt to create a somewhat automated system for creation of visual content.
